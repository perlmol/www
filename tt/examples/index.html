PerlMol - Examples

Here are some sample scripts using PerlMol. Most of them have been submitted or suggested by PerlMol users; if you have something that you can share and could be educational, please send it (itubert@users.sourceforge.net)! The ideal example is relatively short (about 100 lines), self-contained, and does something potentially useful.

This scripts, along with some sample input and output files, are included in the PerlMol bundled distribution under the examples/ directory.

  • file_conversion
  • molgrep
  • pdb_viewer
